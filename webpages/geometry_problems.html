Problem Solving with Geometry:Divide and Conquer

What is the divide and conquer approach? The divide and conquer approach is reading a problem and divinding it into smaller Problems so it can be solved one at a time into the main problem.

Problem Solving Approach


To solve geometry problems effectively, follow these steps.
  1. 1. Read the questions carefully and extract key information
  2. 2. Divide the problems into smaller ones
  3. 3. Form equations based on the key information given
  4. 4. Solve and Check your answer
